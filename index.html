Le Village by CA

An innovative platform to connect startups, mentors, and opportunities.


Access the application

Every year, Village by CA helps around ten startups to develop their business in the professional market.
Today, the new Village by CA Laval website allows you to discover in detail this association dedicated to the success of tomorrow's businesses.

Key features

A showcase for the association and its startups

Offer an exclusive directory of startups selected and supported by the Village by CA in Laval.
Highlight their innovative projects, areas of expertise, and ambitions.
Each profile becomes a window onto entrepreneurship in the region.

A platform facilitating communication

Share the latest news from the Village and the Mayenne ecosystem.
Interviews, feedback, trends and upcoming events.
Turn your blog into a true local newspaper.
Visitors will also be able to easily contact you via a contact form.

A customizable space for Village by CA and its members

Each supported startup has a dedicated space on the platform: presentation page, contact details, link to its website.
The administrator also has their own space to customize all the information on the association's website, so that your visitors are always kept up to date.

About the project

Requested by Amandine Chemin, this project aims to create a showcase website for the Village by CA in Laval, making it a reference for startups looking to join the association, and expand their online presence.

We chose this project because it addresses a real and urgent need that will have a tangible impact.

This work is also part of a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
